    The Story

    We reckon that most people spend too much time doing what they have to do, not what they want to do.

    Well that was us a few years back and so we took the plunge (and felt like the shivering man) to do what we really wanted – to run a brewery with mates.

    And that’s what Coldstream Brewery is all about, taking the time to do what you want to do, catch up and relax with your mates and remember, it’s just life.

    The Pub

    Visit the home of the Shivering Man in Coldstream - the gateway to the Yarra Valley. Enjoy a tasting in the Brewery Bar, the site of our original brewery, or catch up with mates over lunch or a tasting paddle or a pint in the Pub.

    Starring seasonal Yarra Valley produce our menu also features Coldstream beer and cider – look out for our Original Ale bread, Cider basted chicken and Czech Pilsner onion rings.

    Open 365 days a year from 11am – we look forward to seeing you in the Yarra soon.

    The Beer

    The boys started out brewing in what is now The Brewery Bar. A lots changed since 2007 – they’ve added three new beers to their line-up plus a cider and they’re now available nationwide.

    Brewing no longer happens onsite but the beers still have the same high quality hops, malt and yeast, and are still brewed to the original recipes that were trialled, tested and tasted (many a time) out at the Pub.

    There’s no better way to honour a former Brewery than to turn it into a Bar so come pay your respects and have a brew or two.

    See tasting notes in The Store below.

    The Cider

    Made from 100% Victorian apples, our cider is pure goodness, no concentrates or powders here.

    Our apples are crushed and then cold fermented using champagne yeast (essentially the magical turning apples into alcohol process happens in the cold) – giving our cider its delightful fizz. We finish with a filter to leave our Coldstream Cider crystal clear and that’s it.

    Coldstream Cider – clean, crisp and ready to drink. More tasting notes below.
